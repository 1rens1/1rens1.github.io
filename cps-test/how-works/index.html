How the Cps Tester Work.

First of all, when you click the button, the tester starts collecting how many clicks you click the button. Then, when the tester have all of the information it needs, it calculates the cps. How to calculate cps: First you get the amount of clicks and the seconds, then divide clicks by seconds, and there, it's your cps!

Note: This cps tester is not exactly accurate to your average cps.
